Comparison of Evernote and Synapbook

2018-03-03 1 min read Synapbook

Common features

  • Cloud Synchronization enables you to access memo contents created by PC and mobile phone anywhere
  • Data is stored locally so that it can be accessed even during fast search and network disconnection
  • Supports sorting and tagging of notes
  • Support for inserting images into notes

Strengths of Evernote versus Synapbook

  • iOS support
  • Extremely powerful Web scrap functionality
  • Content encryption support
  • It is a stable application that has a long history and many users use it.

[Evernote](https://evernote.com)

Synapbook strength compared to Evernote

  • Ability to create contents with tree structure like mind map
  • Separate simple memo and more riched note to suit your needs
  • Support table of contents structure and draw boxes in groups for effective emphasis
  • Multi-level note classification support
  • All editing keys can be customized

Synapbook

Both products hava different advantages. Take advantage of products that are right for you. :-)
